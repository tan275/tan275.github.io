Satya Muley and the team of Satya Muley & Associates has taken up the task to assist the Police Department & various other Government authorities in Pune to keep an entire region in Pune Corona free. On 15th of April 2020, a direct outreach was performed to approximately six thousand residents of the neighborhoods of Aundh, Baner, Balewadi & Pashan in western Pune to spread the awareness about the government directives, various notifications etc . As per the directives of the Pune Police, the team also undertook efforts to identify 25 volunteers from the responsible citizens of this region who are to be appointed as Special Police Officers by the Pune Police for the period of Corona Pandemic. The team will assist the Police force to implement various directives and spread awareness about measures to be implemented in the region to keep the region safe from risk of Covid 19 pandemic.
