Attention Span Detection

Evaluating Attention Span by detecting Head Pose of Students during Online Classes

Collaborated With: Reha Dhawan, Sreh Deep, and Suhanee Mimani.

  • Python
  • OpenCV
  • Numpy
  • Image Processing

Google Drive
GitHub


In order to enhance students' understanding of concepts and optimize teaching methods, it is essential for teachers to possess the capability to observe students' behavior and discern meaningful indicators to accurately gauge their involvement in learning activities. By effectively monitoring students, educators can derive valuable insights that contribute to a deeper comprehension of the subject matter, fostering an environment conducive to improved learning outcomes and more efficient teaching methodologies.

By combining OpenCV's face detection and pose estimation capabilities with NumPy's array operations and mathematical functions, we can efficiently calculate head pose detection using Euler angles. This information can then be used to make decisions regarding a child's level of attentiveness. It is important to note that accurate results may require sufficient lighting conditions and careful calibration of the pose estimation algorithms based on the target environment.
